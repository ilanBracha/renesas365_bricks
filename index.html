About

Renesas Bricks

A concept hardware-architecture builder. Describe a project, get a compatible, Renesas-first parts stack, see it as a live bus-level block diagram, validate it with automated design checks, and export a costed bill of materials.

What it does

  • Turns a plain-English brief plus constraints into a recommended build
  • Visualises the system as a diagram, brick layout, outline or wiring table
  • Runs design checks across buses, GPIO budget, voltage levels and power draw
  • Surfaces Renesas parts first, with the rationale for each
  • Exports a branded one-page summary, a BOM CSV and a PNG diagram

Built on the Renesas design system

Renesas Blue, the Brand Book secondary palette, Arial / Arial Narrow typography and the squared, flat shape language — applied consistently across every screen.
